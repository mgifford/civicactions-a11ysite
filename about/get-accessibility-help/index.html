Get Accessibility Help

Whether you're facing specific accessibility challenges or need general guidance, here are the best ways to get expert help and connect with the accessibility community.

For CivicActions Team Members

Internal Resources

  • Accessibility Practice Team: Reach out through internal Slack channels for consultation
  • Champions Network: Connect with Accessibility Champions across practice areas
  • Training Resources: Access internal training materials and workshops
  • Project Consultation: Request accessibility reviews and guidance for client work

Office Hours and Support

  • Weekly office hours with accessibility practice leads
  • Project-specific consultation for implementation guidance
  • Code review support for accessibility compliance
  • Client communication assistance for accessibility requirements

For External Organizations

Accessibility Consulting

CivicActions provides accessibility consulting services for:

  • Government agencies implementing Section 508 compliance
  • Nonprofit organizations improving digital inclusion
  • Private companies meeting WCAG standards
  • Educational institutions ensuring accessible learning platforms

Contact: Reach out through our contact form or hello@civicactions.com

Training and Workshops

We offer:

Emergency Accessibility Support

Critical Issues

For urgent accessibility barriers affecting real users:

  1. Document the issue with screenshots and user reports
  2. Implement quick fixes if possible (hiding inaccessible content, providing alternatives)
  3. Contact accessibility experts for guidance on permanent solutions
  4. Communicate with users about timeline for resolution

Legal Compliance Concerns

If facing potential legal action or compliance deadlines:

  • Accessibility audit to identify all issues
  • Remediation plan with realistic timelines
  • Legal consultation with accessibility law specialists
  • User communication about accessibility commitment and progress

Cost-Effective Help Options

Free Resources First

Start with free community resources and open source tools:

  • Documentation and guides from established organizations (WebAIM, W3C)
  • Automated testing tools catch common issues quickly
  • Community forums provide expert guidance at no cost
  • Open source solutions for common accessibility challenges

Paid Services When Needed

Consider professional services for:

  • Complex implementations requiring specialized expertise
  • Legal compliance with tight deadlines
  • Large-scale remediation projects
  • Training and capability building for internal teams
