Mike Gifford

Senior Strategist, Accessibility at CivicActions

About

Mike Gifford is a leading voice in open source accessibility and civic technology. As Senior Strategist for Accessibility at CivicActions, he works to make government digital services more inclusive and accessible to all citizens.

Expertise

  • Open Source Accessibility: Core contributor to Drupal's accessibility improvements
  • Government Digital Services: Specializing in Section 508 compliance and federal accessibility requirements
  • Community Building: Active in accessibility communities and conference speaking
  • Technical Leadership: Bridging the gap between accessibility theory and practical implementation

Notable Contributions

Open Source

  • Long-time Drupal accessibility maintainer and core contributor
  • Contributor to various open source accessibility projects
  • Advocate for accessibility in the broader open source ecosystem

Speaking and Advocacy

  • Regular speaker at accessibility and civic tech conferences including FOSDEM, DrupalCon, and government digital service events
  • Interviewed on accessibility podcasts including AT Banter
  • Published extensively on accessibility in civic tech contexts

Government Work

  • Lead accessibility strategist on federal agency digital transformation projects
  • Contributor to accessibility policy development and implementation
  • Advocate for "accessibility as code" approaches in government technology
