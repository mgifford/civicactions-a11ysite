Home › Champions › Operations

Operations leaders keep accessibility sustainable by standardizing processes, tooling, and support.

Operations actions

  • Bake accessibility checks into definition of done, release checklists, and incident response.
  • Maintain component libraries and design systems with documented accessibility patterns.
  • Track accessibility defects with SLAs and triage rules so regressions are fixed quickly.
  • Ensure vendors and contractors follow the same standards and reporting cadence.
