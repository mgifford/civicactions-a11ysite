Training and Certifications

Building accessibility expertise through structured learning, certification programs, and hands-on practice.

Why Accessibility Training Matters

Accessibility training builds the knowledge and confidence needed to create inclusive digital experiences. Whether you're just getting started or looking to deepen your expertise, structured learning accelerates your impact.

Professional Certifications

Industry-Recognized Credentials

CPACC (Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies)

  • Entry-level certification covering accessibility fundamentals
  • Offered by IAAP (International Association of Accessibility Professionals)
  • Best for: Anyone new to accessibility field

WAS (Web Accessibility Specialist)

  • Technical certification for web accessibility implementation
  • Requires hands-on coding and testing experience
  • Best for: Developers and technical implementers

CPWA (Certified Professional in Web Accessibility)

  • Advanced certification combining technical and strategic knowledge
  • Requires significant accessibility experience
  • Best for: Accessibility leads and consultants

Certification Benefits

  • Validates accessibility knowledge and skills
  • Enhances career opportunities in accessibility field
  • Demonstrates organizational commitment to inclusion
  • Provides structured learning pathway
